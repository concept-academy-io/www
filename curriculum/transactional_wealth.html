Transactional Wealth

From Pre-k to Revenue

Our comprehensive Trade and Work curriculum prepares students for financial literacy and entrepreneurial skills from a young age.

In early years, the curriculum introduces basic economic concepts like Shared Resources, Barter and Trade, as well as Simple Budgeting. Students are taught how trade systems originated and are given basic tools to manage finances.

Moving to Grades 3-5, the focus shifts to Money & Credit and Basic Banking, alongside an Introduction to Budgeting. Here, students learn about the importance of credit and get a hands-on understanding of managing bank accounts.

For Grades 6-8, the curriculum advances to include Customer Centric Service and Upsells, Mechanics of Accounting, and Taxes. Students are equipped with the skills required to understand customer service, balance sheets, and the basics of taxation.

Finally, high school students are offered modules on Online Marketplaces, Business Planning, and Passive Income Strategies. This stage equips them for the challenges of digital business environments, strategic planning, and innovative income sources.

Pre-K to K (Ages 4-6)
  • Economics of Sharing - When someone in your circle has, gains, or succeeds, it enriches the whole community. Emphasizes the mutual benefits of sharing resources and knowledge for mutual benefit.
    • Grades 1-2 (Ages 6-8)
    • Barter - Understand the history and mechanics of trade without money.
    • Basic Trade - Grasp the fundamentals of trading goods and services.
    • Simple Budgeting - Learn to manage finances with basic budgeting techniques.
    Grades 3-5 (Ages 8-11)
    • Money & Credit - Explore the role and management of money and credit.
    • Basic Banking - Get acquainted with banking services and account management.
    • Introduction to Budgeting - Master the basics of income and expense planning.
    Grades 6-8 (Ages 11-14)
    • Customer Centric Service and Upsells - Learn to improve sales through customer-focused strategies.
    • Banking - Delve into advanced banking services and regulations.
    • Mechanics of Accounting - Understand the processes behind financial record-keeping.
    • Taxes - Learn the basics of tax codes, filings, and deductions.
    • Balance Sheet Reading - Acquire skills to interpret and analyze balance sheets.
    Grades 9-12 (Ages 14-18)
    • Understanding Online Marketplaces - Navigate the world of e-commerce platforms.
    • Practical Business Planning - Develop actionable business plans and strategies.
    • Passive Income Strategies - Identify and implement sources of passive income.
    • Content Creation & Monetization - Learn to create and monetize digital content.
    • Negotiation & Persuasion - Master techniques for effective negotiation and persuasion.
    • Hiring Experts - Understand best practices for recruiting skilled professionals.
