Digital Media

From Pre-K to Grade 12

In Pre-K to K, kids start with basic media capture, computer navigation, and child-friendly tech. They also begin documenting monthly highlights.

Grades 1-2 students get a deeper understanding of digital media formats, transitioning from drawings to digital art, and using educational apps.

Grades 3-5 focuses on essential skills like file management and online privacy, introducing simple blogging via Google's ecosystem.

For Grades 6-9, the curriculum dives into Photoshop, video editing, SEO, customer journey mapping, and basic analytics. Students also explore YouTube content creation.

Grades 10-12 students cover search engine marketing, advanced customer acquisition techniques, and data-driven analytics.

Pre-K to K (Ages 4-6)
  • Introduction to Photos and Videos - Learn to capture and view photos and videos on basic devices.
  • Basic Computer Skills - Hands-on tutorials for keyboard, mouse, and basic interface use.
  • Tech: Tablets, Kids Camera - Practice on kid-friendly tablets and cameras with guided activities.
  • Monthly Highlights - Create a monthly digital scrapbook to capture memories.
Grades 1-2 (Ages 6-8)
  • Understanding Digital Media - Discover image, video, and audio formats.
  • Drawings to Digital Art - Learn to scan and color sketches on a computer.
  • Tech: Tablets, Kid-friendly apps - Use educational apps for both learning and play.
  • Monthly Highlights - Compile a digital album that captures your activities.
Grades 3-5 (Ages 8-11)
  • Introduction to File Management - Master folder creation, file sorting, and backup.
  • Privacy Basics - Learn secure passwords and privacy settings.
  • Make a Simple Blog Post - Write and publish a blog post with photos.
  • Tech: Google Docs, Blogger - Learn the basics of Google Docs and Blogger.
Grades 6-9 (Ages 11-15)
  • Photoshop and Video Editing - Basic photo manipulation and intro to video cutting.
  • SEO Basics and Social Media Reach - Understand keywords and social algorithms.
  • Basic Lists & Funnels - Introduction to email marketing and customer paths.
  • Analytics and Tracking - Use basic metrics to measure website or blog success.
  • YouTube Update - Produce a simple video and track its viewership.
Grades 10-12 (Ages 15-18)
  • Photoshop and Video Editing - Advanced techniques including layering and special effects.
  • SEM and Social Media Marketing - Optimize ad spend and target ideal audiences.
  • Advanced Lists & Funnels - A/B testing and data-driven customer segmentation.
  • Analytics, A/B Testing - Evaluate campaign performance and optimize.
  • Polished Media with Analytics - Create professional content and measure ROI.
  • Tech: Analytics, Ad Managers, Advanced Spread Sheeting - Master Google Analytics, learn ad platform management and advanced Excel.
