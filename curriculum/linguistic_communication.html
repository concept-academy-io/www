Linguistic Communications

From Pre-K to Grade 12

Our Language and Communications curriculum offers a robust and age-appropriate learning journey from Pre-K to Grade 12. Beginning with the basics of communication and alphabet recognition, the curriculum is designed to progressively build essential skills. For younger students, the focus is on mastering foundational literacy and simple technology usage.

As students advance to Grades 3-5, the curriculum widens to include complex reading material and digital tools like email and Google Docs, seamlessly integrating tech literacy. These middle years lay the groundwork for effective communication, offering a blend of creative outlets like comic strip creation and journalistic skills.

In high school, the program matures into advanced communication strategies, tackling real world techniques in the digital age by covering advanced tech tools, email automation, and social media management. The program also features real-world applications like campaign tracking and fact-checking, thereby ensuring that students are well-prepared for practical challenges in communication.

Pre-K to K (Ages 4-6)
  • Basic Communication - Master the essentials of verbal and non-verbal cues.
  • Reading: Alphabet Recognition - Start your literacy journey by mastering the ABCs.
  • Tech: Tablets, Kids Camera - Get comfy with kid-friendly gadgets and cameras.
Grades 1-2 (Ages 6-8)
  • Reading and Penmanship - Enhance reading skills while perfecting your handwriting.
  • Basic Grammar - Nail the building blocks of well-structured sentences.
  • Tech: Tablets, Kid-friendly apps - Navigate apps designed for fun and education.
  • Storytelling with Pictures - Illustrate your stories to make them come alive.
  • Emoji Sentences - Express emotions and ideas using fun emojis.
Grades 3-5 (Ages 8-11)
  • Cursive - Add flair to your writing by learning cursive script.
  • Effective Messaging - Become a pro at getting your point across.
  • Advanced Reading - Tackle challenging texts for enriched vocabulary.
  • Tech: Email, Google Docs, Blogger - Communicate like a pro using digital tools.
  • Create a Comic Strip - Blend art and narrative in your own comic adventures.
  • News Reporter - Hone clarity and focus by acting as a junior reporter.
Grades 6-9 (Ages 11-15)
  • Persuasion and Strategy - Learn the art of convincing and strategic thinking.
  • Advanced Grammar - Break down complex sentences and styles.
  • Tech: Email automation tools, Google Sheets - Streamline your communication with tech.
  • Debate Club - Argue effectively and respectfully in mock debates.
  • Social Media Campaign - Run and assess your own mini-campaigns online.
  • Fact Checking - Become a detective of truth in a world of information.
  • Write an e-book - Be an author and share your stories or knowledge.
Grades 10-12 (Ages 15-18)
  • Advanced Communication Strategies - Elevate your communication to new heights.
  • Campaign Tracking & Tuning - Monitor and optimize your communication efforts.
  • Language Arts: Analyze Literature, Write Essays - Dissect classics and craft compelling essays.
  • Tech: Advanced email platforms, social media management tools - Master tools for digital-age communication.
  • Podcast Series - Be the voice behind informative and entertaining podcasts.
  • Analyze Advertisements - Decode the science and art behind persuasive ads.
