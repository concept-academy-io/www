MEP Academic Chemistry

Pre-K to K (Ages 4-6)
  • Introduction to Science: Observing and Describing
  • Basic States of Matter: Solid, Liquid, Gas
  • Mixing Colors: Intro to Chemical Reactions
Grades 1-2 (Ages 6-8)
  • Properties of Water: Sink or Float, Evaporation
  • Introduction to Solids, Liquids, Gases
  • Acid/Base Reactions with Household Items
Grades 3-5 (Ages 8-11)
  • Introduction to Elements and Compounds
  • Chemical Symbols and Formulas
  • Chemical Reactions: Combining and Breaking Apart
  • pH Levels: Acids and Bases
Grades 6-9 (Ages 11-15)
  • Periodic Table: Elements and Families
  • Atomic Structure: Electrons, Protons, Neutrons
  • Chemical Equations: Balancing and Types
  • Conservation of Mass
  • Types of Reactions: Combustion, Synthesis, Decomposition
Grades 10-12 (Ages 15-18)
  • Chemical Bonding: Covalent, Ionic
  • Chemical Thermodynamics: Energy Changes in Reactions
  • Organic Chemistry: Hydrocarbons, Functional Groups
  • Stoichiometry: Moles, Mass, and Reactions
  • Gases: Laws and Behavior
  • Acids and Bases: Strong vs Weak, Calculations
  • Redox Reactions: Oxidation Numbers, Balancing
  • Spectroscopy and Analytical Techniques
