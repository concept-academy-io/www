Grades 6-8 (Ages 11-14)

Applied Math
  • Pre-Algebra and Algebra:
    • Integers/Rationals - Master operations with integers and rational numbers.
    • Algebra Intro - Learn algebraic expressions and simple equations.
    • Linear Equations - Solve linear equations and inequalities.
  • Geometry Mastery:
    • Advanced Geometry - Explore 2D and 3D geometric concepts.
    • Transformations - Understand geometric transformations and symmetry.
    • Complex Shapes - Calculate volume and surface area for complex shapes.
  • Applied Math - Engineering Concepts:
    • Math in Engineering - Apply math in structural and mechanical contexts.
    • Engineering Drawing - Learn basic drawing and measurement skills.
    • Prototypes - Create and test basic engineering prototypes.
Concept Prototyping
  • Detailed Models - Build lifelike models that solve real-world problems.
  • Mechanical Principles - Unlock the principles behind robots and machines with VEX Robotics.
  • TinkerCAD Projects - Layer on complexity with Arduino microcontrollers.
  • Python Introduction - Step into the world of coding with beginner-friendly Python exercises.
  • Applications - Apply your Arduino skills to create robotic toys and gizmos.
Craftsmanship Commercialization
  • Robotics - Advances to building and programming robots, including basic AI behaviors.
  • Advanced Coding - Dives into Python and JavaScript, exploring data manipulation and web development.
Digital Media
  • Photoshop and Video Editing - Basic photo manipulation and intro to video cutting.
  • SEO Basics and Social Media Reach - Understand keywords and social algorithms.
  • Basic Lists & Funnels - Introduction to email marketing and customer paths.
  • Analytics and Tracking - Use basic metrics to measure website or blog success.
  • YouTube Update - Produce a simple video and track its viewership.
Global Studies
Un-ordered Listing due to Interdependence and Age Appropriateness
  • Continents and Tectonic Plates - Examines the movement and formation of the Earth's crust.
  • Countries - All students must learn all the countires of the world and be able to locate them on a blank map.
  • Simple Resources - Overview of essential natural elements and how they sustain life.
  • Global Resources - Discusses the management and sustainability of worldwide resources.
  • Global Transportation - Studies the logistics and environmental impact of international transport systems.
  • Global Trade - Focuses on economic and policy aspects of trade between nations.
  • Types of Government - Compares democratic, authoritarian, and other governance systems.
  • Basic Religions - Introduces core tenets and practices of major world religions.
  • Politics, Beliefs and Religion - Analyzes how politics and faith intersect and influence each other.
  • Basic Conflicts - Investigates the common causes and solutions for disputes at different scales.
  • In-depth Study of Conflicts - Delves into historical and ongoing global conflicts, examining case studies.
Human Biology
  • Genetics & Traits - Delve into how genes influence physical and behavioral traits.
  • Cell Biology - Explore cells as the building blocks of life, including organelles and functions.
  • Organ Systems - Understand how organs work together in systems like digestive, respiratory, and circulatory.
  • Teen Physical Development - Examine puberty, nutrition, and exercise impacts on physical growth.
  • Self Image & Social Media - Evaluate how online presence affects self-perception and body image.
  • Reproductive Anatomy - Discuss male and female reproductive systems in depth, including fertilization.
  • Fertility and Menstrual Cycle - Explore hormonal regulation and the menstrual cycleâ€™s role in fertility.
  • Contraception - Study various birth control methods, effectiveness, and responsible use.
  • STD/STI Awareness - Recognize symptoms, prevention strategies, and treatments for STDs/STIs.
  • Cyber Safety - Discuss risks and laws regarding sexting and other unsafe online behaviors.
  • Consent and Boundaries - Learn how to establish and communicate personal boundaries.
  • Healthy Relationships - Explore elements of respectful, honest relationships.
  • Communication Skills - Develop effective ways to discuss sensitive or difficult topics.
  • Trusted Contacts - Learn how to identify and seek help from trustworthy individuals and organizations.
Linguistic Communication
  • Persuasion and Strategy - Learn the art of convincing and strategic thinking.
  • Advanced Grammar - Break down complex sentences and styles.
  • Tech: Email automation tools, Google Sheets - Streamline your communication with tech.
  • Debate Club - Argue effectively and respectfully in mock debates.
  • Social Media Campaign - Run and assess your own mini-campaigns online.
  • Fact Checking - Become a detective of truth in a world of information.
  • Write an e-book - Be an author and share your stories or knowledge.
Music Platforms
  • Advanced Instrumentation - Proficiency in chosen instrument or software.
  • Collaboration - Group projects for song production.
  • Introduction to Media - Basics of creating music videos and podcasts.
Transactional Wealth
  • Customer Centric Service and Upsells - Learn to improve sales through customer-focused strategies.
  • Banking - Delve into advanced banking services and regulations.
  • Mechanics of Accounting - Understand the processes behind financial record-keeping.
  • Taxes - Learn the basics of tax codes, filings, and deductions.
  • Balance Sheet Reading - Acquire skills to interpret and analyze balance sheets.
