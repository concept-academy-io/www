Grades 10-12 (Ages 15-18)

Applied Math
  • Algebra II and Beyond:
    • Quadratics/Exponentials - Explore quadratic and exponential functions.
    • Equation Systems - Work with systems of linear and nonlinear equations.
    • Trigonometry Intro - Learn basic trigonometry and advanced algebraic concepts.
  • Calculus and Advanced Mathematics:
    • Differentiation/Integration - Introduction to fundamental calculus topics.
    • Limits/Continuity - Explore mathematical limits and continuity.
    • Advanced Math - Study matrices, vectors, and complex numbers.
  • Applied Math - Online Marketing and Statistics:
    • Marketing Stats - Master data collection and analysis in marketing.
    • SEO/SEM Stats - Apply statistical methods in SEO and SEM strategies.
    • Ad Algorithms - Understand the mathematics behind online ad algorithms.
Concept Prototyping
  • Machine Construction - Build functional machines from open blueprints by Open Source Ecology.
  • CAD Mastery - Perfect your TinkerCAD skills.
  • Complex Coding - Tackle advanced coding projects for robust Arduino implementations with assistance from AI.
  • Material Studies - Investigate materials to choose the best for your projects.
Craftsmanship Commercialization
  • Mastering Mediums - In-depth studies in preferred art mediums.
  • Portfolio Building - Creating a diverse art portfolio.
  • Artistic Branding - Developing an artist's persona and self-presentation.
  • Exhibitions & Sales - Organizing art exhibits and understanding the business side.
  • Digital Presence - Building an online portfolio and using social media for promotion.
Digital Media
  • Photoshop and Video Editing - Advanced techniques including layering and special effects.
  • SEM and Social Media Marketing - Optimize ad spend and target ideal audiences.
  • Advanced Lists & Funnels - A/B testing and data-driven customer segmentation.
  • Analytics, A/B Testing - Evaluate campaign performance and optimize.
  • Polished Media with Analytics - Create professional content and measure ROI.
  • Tech: Analytics, Ad Managers, Advanced Spread Sheeting - Master Google Analytics, learn ad platform management and advanced Excel.
Global Studies
Un-ordered Listing due to Interdependence and Age Appropriateness
  • Continents and Tectonic Plates - Examines the movement and formation of the Earth's crust.
  • Countries - All students must learn all the countires of the world and be able to locate them on a blank map.
  • Simple Resources - Overview of essential natural elements and how they sustain life.
  • Global Resources - Discusses the management and sustainability of worldwide resources.
  • Global Transportation - Studies the logistics and environmental impact of international transport systems.
  • Global Trade - Focuses on economic and policy aspects of trade between nations.
  • Types of Government - Compares democratic, authoritarian, and other governance systems.
  • Basic Religions - Introduces core tenets and practices of major world religions.
  • Politics, Beliefs and Religion - Analyzes how politics and faith intersect and influence each other.
  • Basic Conflicts - Investigates the common causes and solutions for disputes at different scales.
  • In-depth Study of Conflicts - Delves into historical and ongoing global conflicts, examining case studies.
Human Biology
  • Nutrition and Digestion - Investigate the digestive process and nutritional needs for different activities.
  • Immune System - Learn about how the immune system fights off diseases and infections.
  • Hormones and Endocrine System - Study hormonal imbalances and effects on mood and development.
  • Reproductive System - Dive deep into reproductive anatomy, processes, and possible dysfunctions.
  • Health and Aging - Discuss how habits now will influence health in later years.
  • Emotions and Mental Health - Investigate the neuroscience behind emotions and mental well-being.
  • Substance Awareness - Examine effects and legal ramifications of drug and alcohol abuse.
  • Stress Management - Explore various coping mechanisms for handling stress effectively.
  • Media and Body Image - Evaluate media's impact on body image and self-esteem.
  • Self-Esteem and Mental Health - Understand the interrelation between self-esteem, mental health, and coping strategies.
  • Teen Pregnancy - Discuss options, consequences, and support systems related to teen pregnancy.
  • STD/STI Information - Explore identification, treatment, and prevention of various STDs/STIs.
  • Safe Practices - Learn about regular health check-ups, vaccinations, and open communication with healthcare providers.
  • Communication and Consent - Study legal and ethical aspects of consent in various situations.
  • Cyber Safety - Understand the long-term risks and potential legal issues with unsafe online behaviors.
  • Date Rape and Abuse - Learn to recognize red flags, preventive measures, and appropriate responses.
  • Effective Communication - Master techniques for respectful and assertive communication.
  • Trusted Contacts - Understand how to vet and select trustworthy contacts for personal and professional support.
Linguistic Communication
  • Advanced Communication Strategies - Elevate your communication to new heights.
  • Campaign Tracking & Tuning - Monitor and optimize your communication efforts.
  • Language Arts: Analyze Literature, Write Essays - Dissect classics and craft compelling essays.
  • Tech: Advanced email platforms, social media management tools - Master tools for digital-age communication.
  • Podcast Series - Be the voice behind informative and entertaining podcasts.
  • Analyze Advertisements - Decode the science and art behind persuasive ads.
Music Platforms
  • Production Skills - Advanced music software and editing.
  • Branding &amp; Presence - Building an artist profile and imagery.
  • Distribution &amp; Monetization - Leveraging music platforms and royalties.
  • Promotion - Using social media and online advertising for music promotion.
Transactional Wealth
  • Understanding Online Marketplaces - Navigate the world of e-commerce platforms.
  • Practical Business Planning - Develop actionable business plans and strategies.
  • Passive Income Strategies - Identify and implement sources of passive income.
  • Content Creation & Monetization - Learn to create and monetize digital content.
  • Negotiation & Persuasion - Master techniques for effective negotiation and persuasion.
  • Hiring Experts - Understand best practices for recruiting skilled professionals.
