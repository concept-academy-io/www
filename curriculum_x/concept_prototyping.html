Concept Prototyping

From Pre-K to Grade 12

This Concepts and Prototyping course is designed to nurture inventive minds from Pre-K all the way to Grade 12. The journey starts at a young age, introducing kids to the fundamentals of mechanics using simple, tactile tools like LEGO and wind-up toys. These experiences help children gain tech comfort, setting them up for a lifetime of ease with technology.

As students advance to Grades 1-2, the course introduces them to the cornerstones of engineering, from simple machines like gears, levers, and pulleys, to beginner-friendly electronics projects. These formative years prepare students for the design challenges they will encounter in later stages.

In Grades 3-5, students solidify their foundational knowledge, learning more complex CAD design, electrical circuits, and basic programming. These skillsets empower them to conceptualize, create, and analyze basic engineering systems, serving as a springboard for more advanced coursework.

Moving into Grades 6-9, the curriculum dives deep into advanced engineering topics like robotics, complex 3D design, and real-world applications of coding and electronics. These form the basis for independent problem-solving, critical thinking, and engineering design.

Finally, in Grades 10-12, the focus shifts to real-world applications. Students engage in machine construction, design optimization, complex coding, and material studies. This immersive experience prepares them for college-level engineering studies and beyond, equipping them with the skills to tackle modern-day engineering challenges.

Pre-K to K (Ages 4-6) - Discovering Mechanics
  • LEGO and Basic Structures - Unleash your creativity by building amazing structures with LEGO.
  • Mechanical Toys - Discover the joy and mechanics behind wind-up toys, cars, and more.
  • Tech Comfort - Gain confidence in navigating educational apps to enhance learning.
  • Kid-Friendly CAD - Turn your ideas into 3D models with easy-to-use TinkerCAD.
Grades 1-2 (Ages 6-8) - Early Engineering
  • Simple Machines - Introduces levers, pulleys, and wheels to demonstrate how force is applied in everyday life.
  • Electronics - Basic circuits, lights, and motors for kids.
  • Arduino and LEDs - Light up your world with beginner-friendly Arduino LED projects.
Grades 3-5 (Ages 8-11) - Building Foundations
  • Mechanical Crafts - Create moving art with water wheels and other handmade mechanisms.
  • Introduction to TinkerCAD - Design and engineering
  • Electrical Circuits - Illuminate the basics of electricity with hands-on Snap Circuit projects.
Grades 6-9 (Ages 11-15) - Advanced Engineering Concepts
  • Thingiverse Exploration - Investigate open-source innovations and spark your own creativity.
  • Mechanical Principles - Unlock the principles behind robots and machines with VEX Robotics.
  • TinkerCAD Projects - Layer on complexity with Arduino microcontrollers.
  • Applications - Apply your Arduino skills to create robotic toys and gizmos.
Grades 10-12 (Ages 15-18) - Real-world Machinery Application
  • Machine Construction - Build functional machines from open blueprints by Open Source Ecology.
  • CAD Mastery - Perfect your TinkerCAD skills.
  • Complex Coding - Tackle advanced coding projects for robust Arduino implementations.
  • Material Studies - Investigate materials to choose the best for your projects.
